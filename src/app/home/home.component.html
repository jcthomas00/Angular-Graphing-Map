Fair and Balanced Weather Data

Extreme weather events including a heavy rain and the snow are known to have negative impacts on the traffic flow and on the air quality in turn. Rainfall however has a moderate effect of washing the pollution in the air as well. This application explores historical data to determine if there really is any relationship between air quality and the quiantity of rainfall.

Impact of Human Activity

Living in a period of strong impact of human activity on the environment and climatic variations, it is reasonable to ask whether the distribution of rainfall between lowland and mountain is stable or not. To this end, rainfall data has been analyzed from around the world.

How is this made?

This site is built as a single-page web application in Angular JS. Services and property binding were used to get data from external sources and pass them within the application.
